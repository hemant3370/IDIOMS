Idioms: no can do -- no guff


 
IdiomMeaningExample
no can do we cannot do it, no way We can't sell you a pet tiger. Sorry, no can do.
no chancy there is no chance of it, it cannot be done When I asked Bill for a loan, he said, "No chancy."
no contest one player or team wins easily, hands down Compare the speed of light to the speed of sound. It's no contest.
no dice no, not approved, no way I asked Dad if we could use the car, but no dice.
no doubt it is certain, without a doubt, no question No doubt, you will inherit his fortune. You are the only heir.
no fair it is not fair, one person or team has an advantage You have six players and we have five. No fair!
no-frills without unnecessary additions or luxuries We just booked a no-frills motel room in Toronto.
no fucking way [B] (See no way)
no great shakes not special, not superior, not so hot I've tasted Granny apples. They're no great shakes.
no guff it is the truth, no kidding, no shit [B] I swam across the lake. No guff.
