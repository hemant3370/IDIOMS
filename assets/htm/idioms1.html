Idioms: 'at a boy (that a boy) -- 'at a girl (that a girl)


 
IdiomMeaningExample
'at a boy (that a boy) good work, well done Whenever I win, Don says, " 'at a boy! Good game!"
'at a girl (that a girl) good work, well done When Judy gets good grades, her mom says, " 'at a girl, Judy!"
